Our Mission

We are on a mission to revolutionize how food is ordered and valued, ensuring zero waste and maximum taste.

The Problem

Thousands of meals are wasted every day due to simple delivery errors like wrong addresses or cancellations. This hurts restaurants and the environment.

Our Solution

Lo'ma provides a platform where these perfectly good, hygienic meals can be resold immediately at a discount to nearby customers. We also operate a top-tier cloud kitchen for fresh orders.
